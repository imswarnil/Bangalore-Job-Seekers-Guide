My Journey in a Nutshell

Every part of my story, from the highs to the lows, has shaped the mission of this project.

Phase 1: The Kota Grind

The journey started with a promise—study hard in Kota, and life will be set. I embraced the challenge with hope and dedication.

Phase 2: The College Detour

Freedom in college led to distraction. A new passion for YouTube emerged, shifting my focus completely away from my engineering degree.

Phase 3: The Failure

Placement season arrived. Unprepared and unfocused, I faced a series of rejections, graduating with a degree but no job.

Phase 4: The New Mission

From the darkest days of feeling lost, a new purpose was born: to build this guide and create a supportive community for everyone on a similar path.
