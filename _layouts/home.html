The Story Behind The Guide

I Graduated as an Engineer. Then I Became Jobless.

My story starts where most success stories end: with failure. I followed the path everyone told me to—Kota, engineering, the whole package. But on graduation day, I had a degree but no job, no plan, and no hope.

That difficult and painful experience taught me a powerful lesson: millions of students in India feel just as lost. I built this guide to be the resource I wish I had during my darkest days. It's built on empathy, for us.
